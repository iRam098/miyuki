Miyuki Commands

Moderation Commands

  • m!ban: Ban the mentioned user.
  • m!kick: Kick the mentioned user.
  • m!purge: Purge Messages (Max 100).
  • m!wchannel: Go to the channel where you want your welcomes to appear and run the command from there.
  • m!say: Bot say your text.

    • List of Commands

    • m!help: Display the list of commands.
    • ping: Check the bot's ping.
